## Summary
Overfilling a U760 transmission can cause fluid aeration (foaming), unstable line pressure, and shudder.

## Signs
- Shudder during acceleration
- Harsh/erratic shifting
- Possible overheating

## Fix
Drain to the correct level using the Toyota level-check procedure.
